Animal FAQs

1. What is the largest land animal?
The African elephant is the largest land animal.
2. How do birds know where to migrate?
Birds use the Earth's magnetic field and the sun for navigation during migration.
3. What's the smallest mammal?
The bumblebee bat holds the title for the smallest mammal.
4. How do chameleons change color?
Chameleons change color using special cells in their skin.
5. Do animals recognize themselves in mirrors?
Some animals, like dolphins and great apes, can recognize themselves in mirrors.
6. What is hibernation?
Hibernation is a winter sleep that helps animals save energy.
7. What's the purpose of a beehive's hexagonal cells?
Hexagonal cells in a beehive store honey and larvae efficiently.
8. How long do domestic cats live on average?
Domestic cats usually live around 15 years.
9. What's the world's fastest land animal?
The cheetah is the fastest land animal.
10. How do sea turtles navigate the ocean for nesting?
Sea turtles use the Earth's magnetic field and the sun for navigation during long ocean journeys.
